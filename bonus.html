Madrid - Marron Bombers

  • The Marron Bombers are the Armored Rider Arms Weapon granted by accessing Jimber Marron Arms
  • They consist of a pair of giant boxing gloves shaped like the spiky outer shell of a chestnut.
  • The Marron Bombers' outer spiky shell can be shed off, revealing two smaller boxing gloves resembling roasted chestnuts.
